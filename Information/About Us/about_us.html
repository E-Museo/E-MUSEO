ABOUT US

E-MUSEO

     E-museo: The website will serve as an accessible and more convenient form of an Art Museum Showcasing the different forms of artworks from renowned and up and coming Filipino Artists. Users will be able to enjoy and ponder on the output produced by our own talented artists.

     Moreover, the website provides deeper knowledge to the users regarding famous and upstarting Filipino artworks and as we know, art can be acclaimed as a manifestation of the environment of the artist as more often than not, artists draw inspiration from its surroundings. Thus, this will also serve as an avenue for the user to gain more information regarding the culture and historical importance these artworks hold.

With the brilliance and creativity of its artists, some of these artworks really made a mark on the nation. Being recognized internationally and becoming attractions for tourists all around the world to see. Most importantly, some of these artworks made an impact so big that it made its marks visible throughout our history.

So, with these all in mind and with a heart ready to ponder on Philippine arts, history, culture, and tradition, be ready to be awed by the brilliance of our fellow countrymen in the field of arts!
