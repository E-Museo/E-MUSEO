PAINTING

Manila Cathedral

INTRAMUROS MANILA

BAROQUE STYLE

The Oblation is a bronze sculpture located in the University of the Philippines Diliman Campus in Quezon City, Philippines. It was created by the Italian sculptor, Francesco Ricardo Monti, and was unveiled in 1935. The sculpture depicts a man, who represents the Filipino youth, holding up an offering or sacrifice towards the heavens with his arms outstretched. The figure is depicted as strong and muscular, with his head thrown back and his eyes closed in an expression of reverence and dedication
