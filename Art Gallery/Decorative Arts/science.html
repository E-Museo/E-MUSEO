SCULPTURE

The Triumph of Science Over Death

Guillermo Tolentino

Realism and Idealism

"The Triumph of Science Over Death" is a monumental sculpture that depicts a female figure holding a torch and a book, representing the triumph of knowledge and enlightenment over death and ignorance. The sculpture showcases intricate details and dynamic movement, with the figure standing on a pedestal adorned with symbolic elements.
